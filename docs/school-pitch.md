# School pitch summary

## One-line pitch
StudyTrack is a local-first study planner for Years 7–12 that helps students capture homework, turn it into realistic study blocks, and show evidence of completion.

## Problem
Students often say:
- they do not know what homework they have
- they forget to record tasks
- they struggle to build a study plan
- they cannot show what work they have completed

## Solution
StudyTrack gives students:
- a homework inbox
- a weekly planner
- a study timer
- a subject hub
- an evidence log
- optional teacher-fed homework posts

## Why it fits school use
- works on iPads and MacBooks
- can operate offline
- supports local-first privacy
- allows bounded AI use for planning rather than answer generation
- can align to school academic integrity expectations

## Rollout
Phase 1: student-only prototype
Phase 2: teacher publishing
Phase 3: optional sync and reporting
Phase 4: bounded AI
